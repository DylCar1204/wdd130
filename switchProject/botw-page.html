Review

This game is one of the most popular games on the Nintendo Switch. Being the very first game released with the Nintendo Switch on opening day, it has marked it's history on the Nintendo Switch. This game is one that changed gaming as a whole because of how open-world it is. Anywhere you see, you can get to, even if it's the tallest mountain! What differentiates this game from any other is the sense of the world feeling so open. The world feels alive from the sounds to the physics engine. The combat is very free as well, allowing the player to choose how he would like to fight. Overall, this game is a MUST for any player interested in adventure or exploration.
